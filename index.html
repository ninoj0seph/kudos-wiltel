About Us

WILTELCOM opened its first branch in October 1999, located at SOUTHSEAS COMPLEX, Cotabato City. Its primary purpose is to cater to the mass market a showcase of different cellular phones and its accessories. With the rapid increase in consumer demand, its 2nd outlet at CST Fashion Mart, still in Cotabato City, was opened.

With the huge potential market in this line of business and considering the increasing “need” of the consumers, WILTELCOM confidently opened its first branch located at Guerrero Street, Davao City, on February 28, 2001.

WILTELCOM has been committed with its quality service to its customers since day one it operated. Continuous patronage from long-time customers made it possible for WILTELCOM to open additional branches located inside major shopping malls in Davao City.

On June 8, 2001, WILTELCOM – 2nd Level Gaisano Mall was inaugurated. Then opened WILTELCOM – 3rd Level SM City Davao on November 16, 2004.

WILTELCOM is equipped with experienced and committed service personnel in their branches. This made its way to be awarded as the Exclusive Service Center of SAMSUNG in Davao City. It served the public on February 21, 2004.

Through the years, WILTELCOM has committed in providing its customers with the best quality products and services. It continues to grow and equip its personnel with seminars and product updates to address proper customer concerns.
